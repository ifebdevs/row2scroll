# Row 2 Scroll (beta)
Transform Excel Rows into Interactive Cards

[Visit the website to learn more about the add-in](https://ifebdevs.com/excel/row2scroll.html)

# General Setup Guide

1. Download the manifest file (manifest.xml) from the website.
   
	a. [https://ifebdevs.com/excel/r2s/manifest-row2scroll.xml]
3. Open Microsoft ~~Excel desktop version or~~ Excel Online.
4. Navigate to the Home tab in the Excel ribbon and look for "Add-ins" on the right side of the ribbon.
5. Click on "More Add-ins" to continue.
6. In the Office Add-ins dialog, click on the "MY ADD-INS" tab next to the "Office Add-ins" title.
7. Click on "Upload My Add-in" on the right side (next to the Refresh button).
8. Click the "Browse" button and navigate to your downloads folder.
9. Select the manifest.xml file you downloaded earlier.
10. Click "Upload" to finish the installation process.
11. After the upload, a new icon labeled "Excel Insights" will appear in your Excel ribbon, allowing you to open the Add-in and complete your practice tasks.
